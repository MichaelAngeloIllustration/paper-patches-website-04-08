City Slickers


Blurb

Inside a radiant, candy-colored metropolis sealed beneath a shimmering magical bubble, life is as lively as it is carefully controlled. The Queen’s enchanted barrier keeps the city safe from the goblins lurking beyond its walls—creatures everyone inside has been taught to fear. Here, becoming a “City Slicker” means everything: elite defenders, dazzling in style and skill, sworn to protect the bubble and its blissful illusion of safety. The streets pulse with energy, humor, and chaos, where friendships are fierce, rivalries are loud, and every encounter matters—because in this city, there’s no such thing as neutrality.

But Celeste is about to turn that world upside down. She’s bold, determined… and secretly a goblin living among those who would fear her most. Training to become the very thing that stands against her own kind, Celeste walks a razor-thin line between two worlds that refuse to understand each other. As she navigates outrageous training trials, suspicious peers, and the ever-looming threat outside the barrier, Celeste must decide who she really is—and who she’s willing to fight for. In a city built on bright lies and buried truths, survival isn’t just about strength… it’s about choosing where you stand when everyone else has already chosen for you.
