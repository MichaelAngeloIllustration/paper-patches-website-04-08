Everyday Grey Has Something to Say


Blurb

Grey can mimic any bird, but can she find her own voice?

In a cozy tree on a sunny hill lives Grey, a little mockingbird with a big talent—she can copy the sound of any bird she hears! From chirping robins to honking geese, Grey mimics them all. But when it's time to sing her own song, Grey discovers she’s forgotten what makes her own voice special.

Join Grey on a heartfelt journey as she learns that being yourself is the most beautiful song of all. With lyrical rhymes, gentle wisdom, and a sky full of sound, this charming tale celebrates self-expression through the music that lives inside every heart.
