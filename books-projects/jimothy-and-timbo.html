Jimothy and Timbo


Blurb

Jimothy and Timbo is a vibrant children’s comic that captures the charm of life’s smallest moments through the adventures of two lovable frogs. Told in delightful 4-panel stories, each comic follows Jimothy and Timbo as they explore their colorful world—splashing in puddles, watching sunsets, or simply sharing a quiet laugh. With gentle humor and heart, the series reminds readers that even the briefest moments can hold the biggest meaning. Perfect for young readers, it’s a celebration of friendship, curiosity, and the beauty of everyday life.
