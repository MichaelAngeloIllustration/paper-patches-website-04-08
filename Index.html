Welcome!

Hi there! My name is Paper Patches, but you can call me Patchy. Welcome to my little Workshop, where paper adventures are always unfolding.

It’s a little rough around the edges here, but that’s exactly how I like it. I create colourful paper collage illustrations that feel like they’re jumping right out of the page and into your hands. Each piece is carefully hand crafted with curiosity, and imagination. Come along with me and discover my Papertopia!

Every story begins with a spark. Whether your story is a tiny spot illustration or a full picture book, your ideas matter deeply to me. We’ll chat about your story, your goals, and your timeline. Tell me about your dreams for the project, and we’ll agree on a plan that’s personalised just for you.

Once we’re on the same page I’ll start sketching away! I’ll sketch and sketch all day! You can lean on my love of colour, composition, and storytelling while staying involved every step of the way. Once you’ve approved of my rough draft and direction, then it’s time for me to bring your final artwork to paper-life!

When you’re ready to bring your ideas to my Papertopia, then I’d love to hear from you. Click the button below and let’s start creating something wonderful together.

Paper Patches Commitments


Creativity

In my Papertopia, every idea is treated with equal consideration and respect. I believe the most meaningful work comes from human collaboration and effort, where each detail is crafted with intention. Every project is given the time, attention, and creative energy it deserves, because art is personal, and it should feel that way.


Curiousity

I let curiosity guide me in everything. Together we’ll investigate every idea by asking questions, exploring possibilities and wandering down unexpected paths. It’s in these moments of discovery that something truly special begins to take shape. By staying open and inquisitive, we uncover the details that make a story feel alive and full of wonder.


Connection

Stories bring us closer together. Through illustration, we share feelings, perspectives, and moments that might otherwise go unseen. I believe art can lend us empathy that remains long after the pages are turned and the book is closed. The most meaningful stories are the ones we carry with us and share with others.

Visit My Papertopia

This is Papertopia, the paper-crafted world I call home.

Everything here, including me, is made up from layers of colour, glue, and imagination. My workshop stands at the heart of it all, powered by the Rock-a-Bye Orb, which transforms dreams from your world into stories of mine. It’s my greatest joy to turn your dreams into my pulp-fiction.

I live a paper life, which means I get to build, shape, and explore this world one illustration at a time. I love welcoming new friends to Papertopia. Every project brings with it new faces, new adventures, and new places to discover! Every character becomes a new neighbour. Every story adds a new layer to my world.

So step inside, take a wander through the paper paths, and imagine the stories we could build together. There’s always room for one more idea in Papertopia.

The Rock-a-Bye Orb

Tucked gently in the heart of my workshop is the rock-a-bye Orb, humming softly as it sways to its own sleepy rhythm. It gathers the quiet dreams drifting out of your world, the bright ideas, the half-formed stories, the little sparks of imagination, and cradles them with care. With a careful mix of colour, texture, and a touch of Papertopia magic, it presses those dreams into something you can see, hold, and share. Every soft clink and whirr is another idea being shaped, another story beginning to unfold.

The rock-a-bye Orb is what keeps Papertopia growing. It stretches the world with every dream it transforms, adding new paths, new places, and new friends along the way. No two creations are ever quite the same, because no two imaginations are the same. That’s the magic of it. Your ideas don’t just pass through the Orb, they become part of Papertopia itself, woven into its paper hills, its painted skies, and the stories that live here forever.
